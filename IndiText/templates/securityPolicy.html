Introduction
This document outlines the security policy for our Translation App.The policy is designed to protect user data and ensure the integrity of our services.
User Data Protection
We are committed to protecting user data.All user data is encrypted both at rest and in transit. We use strong encryption algorithms and secure cryptographic keys.
Incident Response
We have a dedicated security team to respond. We will notify affected users in the event of a security breach.
Third-Party Services
We carefully review all third-party services we use. We only work with partners who also comply with this security policy.
Updates to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any significant changes.
Contact Information
Users can contact us with any questions. Our contact details are available on our website.
Conclusion
